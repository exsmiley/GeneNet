Welcome to Gene Net!

Gene Net enhances gene-targeted therapies by visualizing the intricate connections between around 3000 genes

Features:

Dynamic Gene Network Visualization

See the thousands of connections for yourself. Click your favorite gene and see how other genes are related to it!

Extensive Database

Massive amount of data mined from all over!

Resources

  • Human gene network data was pulled from TRRUST (http://www.grnpedia.org/trrust/)
  • PMIDs for each gene were obtained from this data and then used in PubMed in order to scrape additional data, such as the official name and symbol.
